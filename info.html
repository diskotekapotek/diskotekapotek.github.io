WHO ARE WE?

diskotek+apotek is a worldwide art pharmacy shipping from Sweden, mainly focusing on music. Our current collaborators are:

IBUPROFET PARACETAMOLOW DR. DARK YATZY KLEIN

WHAT DO WE DO?

We release and promote music to visual art. We also host a monthly dance-event at Stockholms badhus called inga fillers bara piller. See our facebook-page for more information about upcoming events

HOW CAN YOU GET INVOLVED?

We welcome demos and artworks aimed at future collaborations with open arms, if you want to collaborate, send stuff to us on diskotekapotek@gmail.com. If you produce, mix or master music and want to be part, we need all the help we can get as everything we are doing is DIY.

We currently take live DJ bookings for clubs in Sweden, if you want to book us please enquire with the above email adress.

I WANNA BUY STUFF

We don't have any reliable distribution channels yet but we have t-shirts, vinyls and posters. If you want to buy anything we'll be happy to post it to you or even bike to your house. Check out our facebook-shop for prices and message us with what you want on Facebook.
